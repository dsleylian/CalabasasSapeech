Pediatric and Adult

We work with all ages, from early intervention to adults. Our clinicians have vast experience with different age groups, as well as with varied areas of need including fluency, speech sound disorders, and language delays.

Family Oriented Approach

"It takes a village to raise a child."
We believe that our clients are part of a community, a family, and in order to see changes in your or your child's speech and language skills, an approach that targets and involves the entire village is required.

In Home Therapy

Research has shown that therapy done in the child's most natural environemnt produces the best results. For most children, this environment is the home. We offer in-home therapy for those interested.
