Meet the team

Goldy Dogsend

MS CCC-SLP

Goldy is a California-licensed Speech Language Pathologist with vast experience with pediatric and adult populations. She is the founder of Calabasas Speech and brings with her years of experience working with children with weaknesses in speech, language, and social skills.

Goldy received her BA in Linguistics from the University of California, Santa Barabara, and her MS in Speech-Language Pathology from California State University, Northridge.

Interested in working with Goldy? Let us know

Puggy Blackfur

MS CCC-SLP

Puggy is an SLP with over 3 decades of experience in acute rehab settings. She has expereince working with adults and pediatrics in inpatient and outpatient settings. Her experience as a medical SLP ranges from dysphagia, neurological disorders, voice, cognition, and complex medical conditions.

Puggy received her MS is Speech and Language Pathology from John Hopkins University.

Interested in working with Puggy? Let us know

Libby Happyface

BS SLP-A

Libby has a specific interest working with the pediatric population and areas of specialty include fluency, articulation, apraxia of speech, social pragmatic disorders, and early intervention. She enjoys working in the early intervention field and helping young children become functional communicators.

Libby received her BS is Speech and Language Pathology from California State University, Northridge.

Interested in working with Libby? Let us know

Flip Fetchinson

BS SLP-A

Flip is the newest addition to our team. He completed his Clinical Fellowship with us in 2019, and now continues to improve the communication skills of his clients. He currently runs our social-skills groups, which are held on Saturdays, and sees individual clients during the week.

Flip received his BS is Speech and Language Pathology from California State University, Northridge.
